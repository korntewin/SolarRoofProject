Solar Forecast

One of the challenges for development of solar power is its intermittent nature. Accurate solar power forecasting is one of the key tools to mitigate challenges created by intermittent nature of energy generation from solar power systems.

DigiSolar makes use of Deep Neural Network (LSTM) models to provide solar forecasting solutions for a multitude of applications. We offer intraday and days-ahead forecasts for individual PV power plants or distributed solar power systems.
